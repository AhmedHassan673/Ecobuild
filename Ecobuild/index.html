About EcoBuild

Balance economic growth with green initiatives. Every decision impacts your city's health!

🎮 How to Play

  • Place Buildings: Click the grid to build structures.
  • Controls: Use Arrow Keys (Left/Right) to switch categories.
  • Save: Your city must be saved manually via the top menu!

⚔️ Core Mechanics

  • Pollution Control: Industry creates pollution. Counter it with Parks and filters.
  • Energy: Power your city with renewables (Solar, Wind).
  • Money: Commercial zones generate income over time.

⚠️ Danger Zones (New!)

  • Decay: If Pollution > 100, buildings may be abandoned!
  • Bankruptcy: If Money < $0, the bank will seize your assets!
  • Levels: High Green Score unlocks advanced tech.
